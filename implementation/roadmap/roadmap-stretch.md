# Runiac Stretch Roadmap
> ⚠️ NOT FOR CURRENT IMPLEMENTATION — This document describes future/stretch scope only.

Track C is deferred future scope. It is separated from MVP routing to prevent future-scope leakage, roadmap drift, and accidental implementation planning.

## Track C Phases

- Phase C1 - Advanced analytics: deeper trend insights after MVP tracking and summary evidence are stable.
- Phase C2 - Expanded training intelligence: adaptive plan improvements after backend validation and governance controls exist.
- Phase C3 - Social and community extensions: optional engagement features after privacy and leaderboard safeguards are proven.
- Phase C4 - External integrations: wearable, map, or third-party integrations after secrets, permissions, and data boundaries are approved.
- Phase C5 - Expert workflow expansion: future expert dashboard capabilities after Platform Administrator approval rules remain enforced.

## Deferred Status

Track C is not active. No Track C phase, capsule, or ADR should be created until the human operator explicitly activates that scope.

## Activation Conditions

- MVP scope is stable enough to evaluate extension impact.
- Required governance, privacy, security, cost, and approval gates are defined.
- The human operator explicitly requests stretch planning or implementation.

AI agents must not plan, implement, or reference stretch features unless explicitly instructed by the human operator.
